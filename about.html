About Tourista PH

Tourist PH is a comprehensive online platform dedicated to showcasing the enchanting beauty, diverse culture, and hidden gems of the Philippines. Our website serves as a digital gateway that immerses travelers in a virtual exploration of the country's captivating destinations. With a focus on promoting sustainable tourism, Tourist PH encourages responsible travel, cultural appreciation, and supports local communities. Moreover, Tourist PH features an exclusive online shop, offering an array of authentic Filipino products, allowing visitors to bring a piece of the Philippines back home.


Our Vision


Our vision is to become the leading platform for travelers seeking to discover the unparalleled wonders of the Philippines. We aim to provide an immersive and educational experience that showcases the country's natural landscapes, vibrant heritage, and vibrant people. By fostering a deep appreciation for Filipino culture and traditions, we aspire to promote sustainable tourism practices that benefit both travelers and local communities alike. We envision Tourist PH as a catalyst for fostering connections, nurturing understanding, and creating unforgettable memories for visitors from all corners of the globe.



Our Mission


Tourist PH is a digital companion that accompanies travelers on their journey of discovery through the breathtaking landscapes and captivating cultures of the Philippines. They curate rich and engaging content that showcases the diverse destinations of the Philippines, encouraging travelers to embark on unforgettable journeys across the archipelago. They are committed to promoting sustainable tourism practices that empower local communities and preserve the country's natural and cultural heritage. Their online shop offers a wide range of Filipino products, handcrafted by local artisans, providing a platform for them to showcase their talents and generate sustainable livelihoods. They also aim to foster a vibrant travel community, providing essential information, practical tips, and comprehensive travel guides to ensure that travelers have a seamless and memorable experience. Tourist PH is more than just a website; it is a digital companion that accompanies travelers on their journey of discovery through the breathtaking landscapes and captivating cultures of the Philippines.
